API KEY
🔑 What's an API key and why do you need one? The AI chat in STRIX is powered by Claude (Anthropic's AI). To use it, the app sends your messages directly to Anthropic's servers from your browser — so you need your own API key to authenticate those requests.

How to get one (free to start):
1. Go to console.anthropic.com
2. Sign up / log in → click API Keys → Create Key
3. Copy the key (starts with sk-ant-) and paste it above

⚠️ Your key stays in your browser only — it's never sent to or stored by STRIX. Anthropic charges per message (~$0.003 each) — very cheap for personal use. New accounts get free credits.

ADHD Focus Hacks
🎧
Brown noise or lo-fi — NOT silence. Your brain needs something to chew on or it'll invent distractions.
📵
Phone in another room, face-down. Out of sight = out of mind (literally — ADHD brains are more affected by visible cues).
🌡️
Slightly cool room (18–20°C). Warm environments suppress dopamine production.
✍️
Write ONE thing you're working on before you start. Your working memory is limited — offload it.
👥
Body doubling works. Work on video call or in a café — another human's presence regulates focus naturally.
